About Our Company

Welcome To Our Hospital

Nimoli Medical Services, which was founded in 2009, has distinguished itself in the delivery of medical knowledge and services and has rightly gained respect as a qualified diagnosis, treatment, and referral center within Nairobi and its surroundings as well as outside of it. We have established leadership in medical operations both in Nairobi and internationally thanks to extensive investment in cutting-edge technology and medical equipment.
